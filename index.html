About Me

I'm Dhruv Sharma, a third-year undergraduate at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign studying physics and mathematics with a minor in computer science. My research interests lie at the intersection of observational astronomy, theoretical cosmology, and modern computational methods — I'm particularly interested in the application of machine learning to accelerate transient detection/classification and large-scale cosmological simulation.

Outside the lab, I enjoy hiking, photography, reading, and anything creative involving working with my hands.

Research Interests

  • Large-scale cosmic structure
  • Cosmological simulation
  • Galaxy formation & evolution
  • Gravitational lensing
  • Time-Domain/Multi-Messenger Astronomy
